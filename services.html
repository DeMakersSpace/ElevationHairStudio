Good to Know

Before You Visit

Tap to expand
We offer a 15-minute grace period for all appointments. Arrivals beyond 15 minutes may be rescheduled or recorded as a no-show at the studio's discretion. Arriving on time helps us give you and every client the experience you deserve.
We require 24 hours' notice for all cancellations or rescheduling. No-shows will incur a non-refundable administration fee. We always encourage rescheduling over cancelling — just reach out and we'll make it work.
Full payment is required before your service begins. All payments are non-refundable. Appointments follow a 2-step confirmation: an automated email upon booking, followed by a personal confirmation via Text, Email, or WhatsApp from our team. Credit card or payment details are held securely to protect against no-shows.
Elevation Hair Studios reserves the right to refuse service or modify any appointment at its discretion. We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and professional environment for every client and staff member.
