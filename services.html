Before You Visit

Late Arrivals
We offer a 15-minute grace period for all appointments. Arrivals beyond 15 minutes may be rescheduled or recorded as a no-show at the studio's discretion. Your barber's time is reserved just for you.
Cancellations
We require 24 hours' notice for all cancellations. No-shows will incur a non-refundable admin fee. We understand things come up — just let us know in advance and we'll happily reschedule.
Payment Policy
Full payment is required before your service begins. All payments are non-refundable. Appointments are confirmed via a 2-step process: automated email followed by personal confirmation via Text, Email, or WhatsApp.
