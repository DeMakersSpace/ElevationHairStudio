> **Policy:** 
Ensuring the Best Experience:
At Elevation, your experience is everything. From the moment you walk through our doors to the final touch of your cut, we're committed to delivering a service that's second to none.

Before your visit, we kindly ask that you take a moment to review our policies — so you know exactly what to expect and we can focus on what we do best: taking care of you.

Have questions? We're always happy to help — just get in touch!

Running Late?:
Life happens — we get it. We offer a 15-minute grace period for late arrivals and will do our best to complete your service in the remaining time. Arrivals beyond 15 minutes may need to be rescheduled or recorded as a no-show, and our standard cancellation fees will apply.

Arriving on time helps us give you and every client the experience you deserve. We appreciate your understanding!

Cancellations & Rescheduling:
We reserve your appointment time just for you, so we kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule.

Missing your appointment or cancelling without prior notice will be marked as a no-show, and a non-refundable administration fee will apply. Please also note that all payments — including deposits and prepayments — are non-refundable.

Whenever possible, we encourage rescheduling over cancelling to avoid any fees and keep your spot secured. We appreciate your understanding and look forward to seeing you!

Appointment Policy:
To ensure every booking is secured and misunderstanding-free, we use a two-step confirmation process. Both steps are required before your appointment is confirmed.

1) ✉️ Email Confirmation — An automated confirmation sent to your inbox upon booking.

2) 💬 Personal Confirmation — A direct follow-up via Text, Email, or WhatsApp from our team.

3) 💳 Payment — Full payment is required before your service begins and is non-refundable.

Elevation reserves the right to refuse service or modify any appointment details at its discretion.
